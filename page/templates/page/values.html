Values

Inclusion

Show up as you are and be an ally to others. By encouraging belonging we will foster a welcoming community that benefits from diversity of thought.

Collaboration

We are all city builders and we know the sum is greater than the parts. We lean on the strengths of one another to meet the challenges of a rapidly urbanizing world.

Trust

We build trust with each other by maintaining a high level of accountability, honesty, and respectful behaviour.

Creativity

In an era of exponential innovation we will harness the power of imagination to uncover remarkable solutions to urban problems.
