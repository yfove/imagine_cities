Our Story

The way we choose to build our cities will be central to our ability to compete as we transition through the 4th industrial period. With finite resources at our disposal, urban leaders are turning to data and science to find the insight needed to make better investments that produce more significant systemic outcomes.

Our Indicators

Supported by our research, our team has produced a list of 20 indicators that we believe are the key drivers behind great city-building. In addition to shedding light on these important components, our indicators shed light on their interdependencies and the importance of systems thinking in meaningfully solving urban problems. These indicators are regularly reviewed and updated. Coming soon.
