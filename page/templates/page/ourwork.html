Programs and Projects

OUR PROJECTS

Short Stories

Throughout the year we curate and show collections of short films from our research repository that highlight interesting perspectives about city building. The topics we choose aim to align with the most important urban issues of the moment, spark discussion, and connect city-builders. Join us at our next event to view, connect and converse.

Year In Review

The Year in Review highlights the best of the best. We pull together the most innovative and unique pieces of research that are solving city problems from our research repository using a defined criteria. To explore this annual collection follow us on Instragram

Speaker Series

Our community of engaged citizens includes many inspiring and creative innovators who challenge the status quo in an effort to build better cities. Our speaker series allows all of us to learn more about city building, celebrate impact and consider how we can each contribute to our city. Sign up for our newsletter!
